# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the principles of the
[Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Expected Behavior

Be respectful, constructive, inclusive, and focused on improving the project.
Accept feedback gracefully, document technical disagreements with evidence, and
respect privacy and confidentiality.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, publishing private information, and
sustained disruption are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Scope and Enforcement

This policy applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and
other spaces where a participant represents the project.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through the contact options on
[the maintainer profile](https://github.com/RNA4219). Reports will be reviewed
confidentially. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may warn,
temporarily restrict, or permanently ban participants when necessary.
